itinerary:
  - day: Day 01
    route: Airport - Pinnawala - Kandy
    description: Upon arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, your representative or designated guide will meet, greet, and escort you to your vehicle. Drive to Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage and enjoy lunch overlooking the elephants. Continue to Kandy with an en-route stop at a Sri Lankan spice garden, where you can enjoy a complimentary head massage. Check in to the hotel, visit a gem gallery, enjoy the Kandyan dance show, and spend the evening around the local market near Kandy Lake.
    stay: Dinner and overnight stay at your selected hotel.
  - day: Day 02
    route: Kandy - Ramboda - Nuwara Eliya
    description: Check out after breakfast and visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic and the National Museum of Kandy. Later, drive up to Arthur's Seat viewpoint for a beautiful view of the city. Continue towards Nuwara Eliya with stops at the Bhakta Hanuman Temple, lunch overlooking Ramboda Falls, and the Damro Tea Processing Factory to experience world-famous Ceylon tea. Reach Nuwara Eliya, check in to the hotel, and spend the evening at leisure.
    stay: Dinner and overnight stay at your selected hotel.
  - day: Day 03
    route: Nuwara Eliya City Tour
    description: After breakfast, visit Seeta Amman Temple and Hakgala Botanical Gardens. Later, visit Gregory Lake, where you can enjoy water sports. Return for lunch and relaxation, then spend the evening visiting Victoria Park and the nearby alleys.
    stay: Dinner and overnight stay at your selected hotel.
  - day: Day 04
    route: Nuwara Eliya - Bentota
    description: Check out after breakfast and drive to Bentota. On route, stop at St. Clair Major Falls viewpoint for a scenic photo stop overlooking the waterfall and tea gardens. Continue your drive to Bentota, enjoy lunch en-route at a local restaurant, arrive at the hotel, and spend leisure time at the beach.
    stay: Dinner and overnight stay at your selected hotel.
  - day: Day 05
    route: Bentota and Galle City Tours
    description: After breakfast, drive to Galle. On route, enjoy a mangrove boat ride on Madu River. Continue to Galle and visit the Dutch Fort and Maritime Museum, then enjoy lunch at a local restaurant. After lunch, drive back to Bentota with a stop at Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery. The evening is free for water sports or relaxation on the beach.
    stay: Dinner and overnight stay at your selected hotel.
  - day: Day 06
    route: Bentota - Colombo City Tour - Airport
    description: Check out after breakfast and drive for a Colombo city tour with time for shopping. Visit Galle Face Green and, if time permits, Gangaramaya Temple by Beira Lake or the nearby Pettah Floating Market. On completion, drive to the airport for departure.
    note: End of the tour.

Island's Wonders gives travelers an easy way to experience Sri Lanka's hill country, waterfall routes, beach stays, southern heritage, and Colombo in a relaxed six-day journey.

- Hill country views and tea country stops
- Bentota beach time and Galle heritage
- Scenic drives with relaxed private transfers
