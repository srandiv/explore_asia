Our B2B service is built for travel agents who need a dependable Sri Lanka ground partner behind their brand. We support travel agent to travel agent work with practical route planning, realistic costing, hotel and transport coordination, and the local knowledge needed to turn an inquiry into a confident proposal.

For each request, we can prepare two clear versions: a polished itinerary for your client and a separate costing sheet for your business. This keeps the client experience smooth while giving you the details you need to manage margins, compare options, confirm inclusions, and continue the sale with confidence.

Trust is the center of our B2B approach. We understand that your relationship with your customer depends on our accuracy, timing, and discretion. Our team works as your local Sri Lanka extension, protecting your client relationship while giving you reliable updates before arrival, during the tour, and after completion.

We also help highlight specialized services that can make your proposal stronger: cultural circuits, wildlife experiences, beach extensions, luxury stays, family-friendly routing, MICE and incentive arrangements, honeymoon touches, guide language preferences, special meals, accessible travel needs, and last-minute operational support when plans change.

Whether you are handling one private couple, a small family, a corporate incentive group, or repeat seasonal business, our goal is to make the partnership valuable enough to continue: clear communication, fair pricing, consistent delivery, and Sri Lanka expertise your agency can confidently sell.
